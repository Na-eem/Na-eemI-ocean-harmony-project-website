Ocean Harmony Project is a community-driven marine conservation initiative based in Winneba, Ghana, dedicated to protecting marine ecosystems and empowering coastal communities.

Our Mission

Protect and restore marine and coastal ecosystems while promoting ocean literacy, empowering youth, and supporting sustainable ocean stewardship through education, research, and community collaboration.

🎓 Ocean Education

Educating communities about marine conservation and climate change through workshops and campaigns.

🤝 Community Engagement

Working directly with coastal residents, fishers, and youth groups to create lasting impact.

🔬 Research & Data

Promoting citizen science and community-based environmental monitoring for better decisions.

🌍 Sustainable Oceans

Advocating for policies and practices that protect marine biodiversity for future generations.
