# 🌊 Ocean Harmony Project

**Ocean Harmony Project** is a community-driven ocean conservation initiative based in **Winneba, Ghana**. The project focuses on protecting marine ecosystems, empowering coastal communities, and raising awareness about sustainable ocean stewardship through education, research, storytelling, and youth engagement.

The initiative promotes harmony between **people, the ocean, and coastal livelihoods**, supporting global sustainability goals such as **United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 14 – Life Below Water**.

---

# 🌍 Project Mission

The mission of the Ocean Harmony Project is to:

- Protect and restore marine and coastal ecosystems.
- Promote ocean literacy and environmental education.
- Empower youth and coastal communities to become ocean stewards.
- Support sustainable fisheries and responsible ocean use.
- Encourage collaboration between communities, researchers, and policymakers.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

The Ocean Harmony Project aims to:

1. **Increase Ocean Awareness**  
   Educate communities about marine conservation and climate change.

2. **Community Engagement**  
   Work directly with coastal residents, fishers, and youth groups.

3. **Environmental Education**  
   Organize workshops, campaigns, and outreach programs.

4. **Research & Data Collection**  
   Promote citizen science and community-based environmental monitoring.

5. **Advocacy for Sustainable Oceans**  
   Encourage policies and practices that protect marine biodiversity.
