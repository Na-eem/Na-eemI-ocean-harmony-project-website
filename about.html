About Ocean Harmony Project

Understanding Our Mission and Vision

Who We Are

Ocean Harmony Project is a community-driven ocean conservation initiative based in Winneba, Ghana. We focus on protecting marine ecosystems, empowering coastal communities, and raising awareness about sustainable ocean stewardship through education, research, storytelling, and youth engagement.

Our Vision

We envision a world where harmony exists between people, the ocean, and coastal livelihoods. Our initiative supports global sustainability goals, particularly United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 14 – Life Below Water.

Our Core Values

  • Community First: We believe in grassroots participation and local empowerment.
  • Ocean Stewardship: We are committed to protecting and restoring marine ecosystems.
  • Education: We promote ocean literacy and environmental awareness.
  • Collaboration: We encourage partnerships between communities, researchers, and policymakers.
  • Sustainability: We support responsible ocean use and sustainable livelihoods.

Why Ocean Conservation Matters

Our oceans are facing unprecedented challenges: climate change, overfishing, pollution, and habitat loss. Yet they remain vital to human survival, providing food, livelihoods, and oxygen for billions. The Ocean Harmony Project recognizes that protecting marine biodiversity is not just an environmental issue—it's a social and economic imperative for coastal communities like Winneba.
