Community Impact

Stories of Change from Winneba

Grassroots Participation at the Heart of Change

The Ocean Harmony Project emphasizes community participation. Volunteers and community members help implement conservation activities that create real, measurable impact in Winneba and beyond.

Our Community Activities

🏖️ Beach Clean-ups

Regular beach clean-up drives organized with community volunteers to remove plastic waste, fishing debris, and other pollutants. These events educate participants about marine pollution while directly protecting coastal habitats.

📢 Environmental Awareness Campaigns

Strategic campaigns focused on ocean conservation, sustainable fishing, and climate change. We use storytelling, social media, and community events to reach diverse audiences.

🎓 School Outreach Programs

Educational sessions in schools to engage youth in ocean conservation. Students learn about marine biodiversity, climate change impacts, and their role as future stewards.

💬 Community Dialogues

Forums and discussions with local fishers, boat owners, and community leaders about sustainable fishing practices and ocean management.

👨‍🎓 Youth Leadership Training

Capacity-building programs for young people, equipping them with skills, knowledge, and confidence to lead conservation efforts in their communities.

🔬 Community Science Projects

Citizen science initiatives where community members collect environmental data, monitor marine health, and contribute to conservation research.
